## What is Rapidly?

Rapidly is a secure file-sharing platform with built-in payment gating. Share files peer-to-peer with end-to-end encryption (AES-256-GCM over WebRTC), monetize your shares via Stripe Connect, and manage everything from a workspace dashboard.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="End-to-End Encrypted Sharing" icon="lock">
    Files transfer directly between peers via WebRTC. AES-256-GCM encryption
    ensures only the intended recipient can access your content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payment-Gated Channels" icon="credit-card">
    Attach prices to file shares and get paid via Stripe Connect. Fixed pricing,
    pay-what-you-want, or free -- your choice.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Problems We Solve

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Secure File Distribution" icon="shield-halved">
    **The Problem**: Sharing files securely -- especially large or sensitive ones -- requires complex infrastructure: S3 buckets, signed URLs, access control, and encryption.

    **Rapidly's Solution**: P2P encrypted file transfers via WebRTC with download quotas and ClamAV malware scanning. No third-party cloud storage needed -- files go directly from sender to receiver.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Monetizing Digital Content" icon="money-bill">
    **The Problem**: Selling access to files means stitching together a payment processor, access control layer, delivery mechanism, and customer management system.

    **Rapidly's Solution**: Create a share, set a price, and distribute the link. Rapidly handles payment collection, access gating, and customer tracking in one platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Secret & Sensitive Data Sharing" icon="user-secret">
    **The Problem**: Sharing passwords, API keys, or sensitive documents via email or chat leaves data exposed in message histories and server logs.

    **Rapidly's Solution**: One-time encrypted secrets with automatic expiration. Once viewed, the secret is destroyed. No trace left behind.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Workspace">
    [Sign up for Rapidly](https://rapidly.tech/signup) using Google, Microsoft, Apple, or email. Create a workspace to organize your shares, customers, and team members.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Share Your First File">
    Create a file-sharing channel from your dashboard:
    - Upload a file or start a live P2P channel
    - Optionally set a price (fixed, pay-what-you-want, or free)
    - Share the generated link with your recipient

    The recipient connects directly to you via WebRTC -- no file ever touches our servers.

    Learn more about [File Sharing](/features/file-sharing)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Stripe (Optional)">
    To accept payments on your shares, connect your Stripe account via Stripe Connect in the workspace settings. Rapidly handles checkout and deposits payouts directly to your bank account.

    Learn more about [Finance & Payouts](/features/finance/accounts)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Webhooks (Optional)">
    Get notified when shares are accessed, payments complete, or customers are created:
    - Configure webhook endpoints in your dashboard
    - React to events in real time
    - Keep your systems in sync

    Read the [Webhooks guide](/integrate/webhooks/endpoints)
  </Step>
</Steps>
