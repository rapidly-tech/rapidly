# Security Policy

Rapidly takes the security of our platform and our users' data seriously.

## Scope

This policy covers the Rapidly open-source repository, the hosted platform at `rapidly.tech`, and all official client libraries published under the `@rapidly-tech` namespace.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security issue, please **do not** open a public GitHub issue. Instead, submit a confidential report through our [GitHub Security Advisory portal](https://github.com/rapidly-tech/rapidly/security/advisories/new).

You can also reach us directly at **security@rapidly.tech**.

## What to Include

- A clear description of the vulnerability and its potential impact.
- Steps to reproduce the issue, including any relevant configuration or environment details.
- Any proof-of-concept code or screenshots, if available.

## Response Timeline

- **Acknowledgement**: within 2 business days of receipt.
- **Initial assessment**: within 5 business days.
- **Fix or mitigation**: as soon as practically possible, with coordinated disclosure.

We will keep you informed throughout the process and credit reporters in release notes unless anonymity is requested.
